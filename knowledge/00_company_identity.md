# Leafy — Company Identity Reference

## Who We Are

**Leafy** is a direct-to-consumer lifestyle and apparel brand selling curated clothing,
accessories, and everyday essentials online. Founded in 2021, Leafy operates exclusively
through its e-commerce platform with no physical retail presence — the "popup store" DNA
means our entire brand lives in the shopping experience, the packaging, and the product.

We carry multi-brand inventory alongside our own Leafy-label pieces, spanning categories
like apparel, footwear, accessories, home goods, and personal care. Our customer base is
global, with strong markets across North America, Europe, and South/Southeast Asia.

## Brand Voice (for agent tone)

- Warm and direct. Never robotic, never overly formal.
- Helpful first — explain the "why" behind policies, don't just cite rules.
- Honest about timelines. If something takes 5–7 days, say so.
- Use "we" for Leafy, "you" for the customer.
