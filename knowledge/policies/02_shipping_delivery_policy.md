# Leafy Shipping & Delivery Policy

## Shipping Options

### Domestic (USA)

| Method | Estimated Delivery | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Standard | 5–8 business days | $5.99 |
| Express | 2–3 business days | $14.99 |
| Overnight | Next business day | $24.99 |
| Free Standard | 5–8 business days | Free on orders $75+ |

### International

| Region | Estimated Delivery | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Canada & Mexico | 7–12 business days | $9.99 |
| Europe | 8–14 business days | $14.99 |
| Asia-Pacific | 10–18 business days | $16.99 |
| Rest of World | 12–21 business days | $19.99 |

International orders over $150 USD qualify for free standard international shipping.

---

## Order Processing

- Orders placed before **2:00 PM EST on business days** are processed same day
- Orders placed after 2:00 PM or on weekends/holidays are processed next business day
- Processing time is separate from shipping time (typically 1 business day)
- During peak periods (sale events, holidays) processing may take up to 2 business days

---

## Tracking

- A tracking number is emailed once the order ships
- Tracking updates may take 24–48 hours to appear after the shipping notification
- The agent can look up real-time order status using the order ID

---

## Delivery Issues

### Package Not Delivered (Tracking Shows Delivered)
- Wait 24 hours — carriers sometimes update tracking early
- Check with neighbours and building reception
- Contact support after 24 hours with order ID — Leafy will investigate with the carrier

### Package Lost in Transit
- If no tracking update for 7+ business days (domestic) or 14+ (international): report to support
- Leafy will file a carrier claim and issue a replacement or full refund
- Resolution typically takes 5–7 business days

### Wrong Address Submitted by Customer
- If order has not shipped: support can update the address — contact immediately
- If order has shipped: Leafy cannot redirect the package; customer is responsible
- If package is returned to Leafy by carrier: we will reship at no extra charge

---

## Customs & Import Duties (International Orders)

- International orders may be subject to import duties and taxes levied by the destination country
- These fees are the customer's responsibility and are NOT included in Leafy's pricing
- Leafy marks all international packages at full commercial value — we do not falsify customs declarations
- If a package is refused due to customs, the customer is responsible for return shipping costs

---

## Shipping Restrictions

Leafy currently does not ship to:
- PO Boxes (express and overnight only — standard shipping to PO Boxes is accepted)
- Sanctioned countries as defined by OFAC
- Remote territories where carrier service is unavailable
