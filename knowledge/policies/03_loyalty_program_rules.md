# Leafy Rewards — Loyalty Program Rules

## Overview

Leafy Rewards is our points-based loyalty program. Every purchase earns points,
points unlock tiers, and tiers unlock better perks — including extended return
windows, priority support, and exclusive early access.

---

## Earning Points

| Action | Points Earned |
|---|---|
| Every $1 spent on an order | 10 points |
| Writing a verified product review | 50 points |
| Referring a friend (on their first purchase) | 200 points |
| Birthday bonus (once per year) | 100 points |
| Account creation | 50 points (one-time) |

Points are credited to the account **after the order is delivered and the return
window has closed**. Points from returned items are not awarded.

---

## Tier Structure

Tiers are calculated based on **total lifetime points earned** (not current balance).
Spending points does not reduce your tier progress.

| Tier | Threshold | Badge Colour |
|---|---|---|
| Bronze | 0 – 249 points | Bronze |
| Silver | 250 – 749 points | Silver |
| Gold | 750 – 1,499 points | Gold |
| Platinum | 1,500+ points | Platinum |

Tier status is reviewed monthly. You keep your tier for 12 months from the date
you reached it, even if your activity drops.

---

## Tier Benefits

### Bronze (0–249 pts)
- 10 points per $1 spent
- Standard 30-day return window
- Access to member-only sale events

### Silver (250–749 pts)
- 12 points per $1 spent
- Standard 30-day return window
- Early access to new product drops (24 hours before public)
- Birthday bonus points (100 pts)

### Gold (750–1,499 pts)
- 15 points per $1 spent
- Standard 30-day return window
- Priority support queue (faster agent response)
- Early access to sales (48 hours before public)
- Free standard shipping on all orders

### Platinum (1,500+ pts)
- 20 points per $1 spent
- Extended 45-day return window
- Free return labels on ALL returns (not just defective)
- Dedicated support — top of queue always
- Early access to sales (72 hours before public)
- Free express shipping on all orders
- Exclusive Platinum-only products and bundles

---

## Redeeming Points

Points can be redeemed at checkout as a discount:

| Points | Discount |
|---|---|
| 500 points | $5 off |
| 1,000 points | $10 off |
| 2,000 points | $22 off (10% bonus) |
| 5,000 points | $60 off (20% bonus) |

- Minimum order value of $20 to redeem points
- Points cannot be combined with other promotional codes (except birthday offers)
- Redeemed points are non-refundable if order is returned — store credit will not include the points-equivalent value

---

## Points Expiry

- Points expire if the account has **no purchase activity for 18 months**
- A warning email is sent 60 days before expiry
- Expired points cannot be reinstated
- Tier status is unaffected by point expiry (based on lifetime earned, not balance)

---

## Account & Points Disputes

If a customer believes points were not correctly awarded:
- Contact support within 30 days of the relevant order/action
- Provide the order ID or relevant transaction
- Agent can view the loyalty ledger and manually credit points if warranted

---

## Program Changes

Leafy reserves the right to modify tier thresholds, point values, and benefits
with 30 days' notice to members. Existing earned points are always honoured.

---

*The Leafy Rewards program is available to customers with a registered Leafy account.
Points have no cash value and are non-transferable.*
